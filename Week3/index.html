Biography of Pollock

Pollock was an american abstract painter. He was known for his very loose drip techniques. He died in 1956, but is still commonly talked about

What does Pollock do while painting?

  • Puts the canvas on the floor
  • Puts music on (sometimes) to get him in the mood
  • Gets his paint and brushes and begins working
  • Mixes the colors he wants ahead of time
  • Gets all into his art, very loose even steping on the canvas
  • Works fast so the paint doesn`t dry up on him
